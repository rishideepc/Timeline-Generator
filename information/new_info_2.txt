2. Regarded as 'Captain Fantastic' by Leicester City FC fans, Foxes legend Steve Walsh has termed the Next Generation Cup, 2022 'a great tournament for Indian youngsters'.
Date: Vimal Mohan | Thursday July 28, 2022
More Info: Regarded as 'Captain Fantastic' by Leicester City FC fans, Foxes legend Steve Walsh has termed the Next Generation Cup, 2022 'a great tournament for Indian youngsters'. The international tournament is part of the Premier League's partnership with Football Sports Development Limited (FSDL) and has five academy teams from England, two from India, and one from South Africa currently participating in the United Kingdom. Walsh, who played for former Premier League champions Leicester City for 14 seasons has advised the emerging players to soak in the experience as much as possible.
